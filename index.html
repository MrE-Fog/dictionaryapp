Instructions: Type a word, double-tap to select, then choose "define".

Note: This requires an iOS device running iOS 5. The web app isn't actually an app at all. I was just bored and decided to make a quick way to get a large textbox to access iOS' built-in dictionary because I thought all the dictionary apps on iPad sucked, especially considering you get the Oxford English Dictionary built-in.
